Caravan Fest

The Caravan Fest is a three-day event organized by the Indian Institute of Science (IISc). It brings together technology enthusiasts, startups, and industry experts for an immersive and informative experience.

Event Details:

  • Date: March 15-17, 2024
  • Location: IISc Campus, Bangalore, India
  • Organizer: Indian Institute of Science (IISc)

Activities:

  • Hackathon: Engage in a thrilling 48-hour hackathon where participants showcase their coding and problem-solving skills.
  • Guest Lectures: Listen to renowned industry experts sharing their insights and knowledge on cutting-edge technologies.
  • Workshops: Attend interactive workshops conducted by experts to learn about the latest trends and tools in technology.
  • Startups Showcase: Explore innovative ideas and projects presented by startups and get a glimpse of the future of technology.
  • Tech Exhibit: Experience hands-on demonstrations of emerging technologies and explore futuristic gadgets and devices.

Don't miss out on this exciting opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals, expand your knowledge, and explore the world of technology!
